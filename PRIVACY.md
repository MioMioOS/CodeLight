# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 6, 2026

## Overview

CodeLight is an open-source application that syncs Claude Code sessions between your Mac and iPhone. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

CodeLight does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

## Data Storage

- **Session data** is stored on your self-hosted CodeLight Server. You control where your data lives.
- **Authentication keys** are stored securely in your device's Keychain.
- **Server URLs** and connection settings are stored locally on your device.

## End-to-End Encryption

Messages between your Mac and iPhone are designed to be end-to-end encrypted. The CodeLight Server acts as a zero-knowledge relay — it stores encrypted data without the ability to read it.

## Third-Party Services

CodeLight does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No data is shared with third parties.

## Camera Usage

CodeLight uses your device's camera solely for scanning QR codes during the device pairing process. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

## Push Notifications

If enabled, push notifications are delivered via Apple Push Notification Service (APNs). Device tokens are stored on your self-hosted server and are not shared with anyone else.
